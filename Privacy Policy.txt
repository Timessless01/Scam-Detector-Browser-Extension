Privacy Policy for Scam Detector
Last updated: [Date]

1. Introduction
Scam Detector ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the Scam Detector Chrome extension.

2. Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. The extension operates entirely on your device and does not transmit any information to external servers.

3. Google Safe Browsing API
The extension uses the Google Safe Browsing API to check URLs for phishing and malicious content. URLs are sent securely to Google for threat analysis but are not stored or logged.

4. Email Scanning
The extension scans email content locally to detect phishing keywords and suspicious links. No email data is transmitted, stored, or collected.

5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
